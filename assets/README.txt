Place your logo files here:
  logo.ico  — Used as the EXE/taskbar icon (32x32 or 256x256 recommended)
  logo.png  — Used in the app header (any size, will be resized to 40x40)

Background image:
  Set any .jpg/.png/.gif as background via Settings tab → Background Image

To convert PNG to ICO (free tool):
  https://convertio.co/png-ico/
